# Ms. Pac-Man Research Memory Bank

This folder is designed as your **working memory + total memory bank** for a 10-minute presentation on *Ms. Pac-Man* from a business-focused lens.

## How to use this folder quickly

1. Start with `docs/01-executive-brief.md` for a complete 10-minute story.
2. Use `docs/02-question-bank.md` to ensure you answered all 10 required class questions.
3. Pull hard numbers and assumptions from `docs/03-business-math.md` for your “do the math” section.
4. Use `docs/04-legal-and-rights.md` for legal battles and Q&A prep.
5. Use `docs/05-platforms-and-releases.md` for platform history.
6. Use `docs/06-presentation-outline.md` to build your PPT slide-by-slide.
7. Use `docs/07-sources-and-confidence.md` to cite sources and handle uncertainty transparently.
8. Use `docs/08-story-deep-dive.md` when you need defendable numbers on team size, man-month cost, and realistic sales/revenue ranges.

## Suggested PPT structure (10 min)

- Slide 1: Title + thesis (“The most successful unauthorized sequel ever commercialized”).
- Slide 2: Timeline and origin story.
- Slide 3: Unique business model and deal structure.
- Slide 4: Platforms and distribution expansion.
- Slide 5: Financial performance and math.
- Slide 6: Legal battles and rights fragmentation.
- Slide 7: Critical/public reception.
- Slide 8: What they did right/wrong.
- Slide 9: Real business story (why this case is unique).
- Slide 10: Q&A prep backup slide.

## Core thesis

*Ms. Pac-Man* is not just a famous arcade game. It is a case study in:
- fan-made innovation becoming a mainstream product,
- licensing/rights complexity becoming long-term strategic risk,
- and how distribution and platform strategy can turn a derivative product into a billion-dollar brand asset.
